Game Rules & Scoring

1. Scoring

  • Response Values: Correct reveals earn 1 to 5 points based on popularity.
  • Point Spread: The most popular response earns 5 points; the least earns 1 point.
  • Total Score: All points are cumulative throughout the entire tournament.

2. Tournament Progression

  • Qualifiers: All the Teams
  • Quarterfinals: Top 8 Teams
  • Semifinals: Top 4 Teams
  • Finals: Top 2 Teams

3. Tie-Breakers

  • Match Ties: Victory is awarded to the team with the highest-point single answer in that match.
  • Tournament Ties: Decided by the total count of 5-point answers, then by the highest Qualifier score.
